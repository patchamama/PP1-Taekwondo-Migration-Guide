Ho-Sin-Sul-Berlin e.V Hapkido Verein aus Berlin Mitte.

The Ho-Sin-Sul-Berlin e.V Hapkido Verein training takes place twice a week. Our training is holistic, so we place great importance on taking into account all areas of Taekwondo and Hapkido. In addition to modern competitive Taekwondo, we also focus on various traditional exercises. We include two form systems (Poomse and Hyong) and, to a large extent, self-defence, especially in Hapkido, in our training. The fun of movement and trying new kicks, punches, levers, throws and hand pad combinations and partner exercises are very important.

Interested in a free taster session to get a feel for what we're like?

Then visit us on Monday or do you have a question for us?

Then write us an email: info@hosinsul-berlin-ev.de
